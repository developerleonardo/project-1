Welcome to Sanivet Veterinary Center

Sanivet Veterinary Center is pleased to serve Medellin, Antioquia and the surrounding areas. Our goal is to provide you and your pets with the best veterinary experience possible. At Sanivet Veterinary Center, everyone is considered a leader and an integral member of the health care delivery team. Every day at Sanivet Veterinary Center, it is our friendly team’s responsibility to help make your veterinary experience a positive one to ensure that both you and your pet are at ease. In addition to mandatory veterinary knowledge and training, our team is selected for kindness, compassion, patience, professionalism, integrity, and agility. We are here for you, we are here for your pet, and we are your neighborhood’s favorite vet. Please don’t hesitate to contact us today at (212) 580-1800 to request your pet’s appointment!
